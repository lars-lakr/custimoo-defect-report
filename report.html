How the Numbers Are Calculated

  • Reporting window is Oct 2025 – Jun 2026*.
  • Total month volume uses proper products only (excludes name plates, fight straps, logo patches, accessories).
  • Defects are tracked from fu@custimoo.com customer feedback emails, with manual overrides for exact counts confirmed by order admins.
  • When email does not specify the exact count, the full order quantity is used as the upper-bound estimate.
  • Factory comparisons use total shipped order quantity per factory from the backend database, bucketed by order_items.status_updated_at for shipped/completed/shipping-status rows.
  • Qarma comparisons use approved, own, final inspection reports bucketed by Inspection end time.
  • Defects are bucketed by the month the customer first reported the issue (fu email received date), not the order creation date.
  • Jun 2026* is still in progress.
  • Click any number in the report to drill into the specific orders behind it.
  • Errors are tracked and estimated from the email fu@custimoo.com.
  • We try to deduce the actual affected number of QTY to keep it fair. The system can sometimes have difficulties handling orders split across multiple factories.
  • If an email is unclear or without order number, the sender will be asked to provide an order number and be a little more clear.
